♻️ Zero Waste Initiative

Turning post-harvest loss into lasting value

A large portion of fish spoils unsold every day. We convert that surplus into premium, long shelf-life products sold directly on the platform.

1

Preservation Processing

Unsold fish is converted using salt, oil and controlled processing into safe, shelf-stable products within hours of collection.

2

Direct-to-Consumer Sales

Products are packaged and listed directly on our platform, eliminating the need for third-party distribution and maximising margin.

3

Additional Revenue Stream

What was financial loss becomes predictable monthly income for participating fishermen and the platform alike.

Food safety standard: All products maintain pH ≤ 4.6 and controlled processing temperatures, meeting national food safety requirements.

How It Works

From catch to customer in four steps

A streamlined, data-driven workflow that replaces phone calls, middlemen and guesswork with transparency and speed.

1

Fishermen List Catch

Verified fishermen post real-time listings each morning — species, quantity, price and location — straight from the boat.

2

Buyers Place Orders

Restaurants, retailers and consumers browse, compare prices and place orders directly — no calls, no haggling, no uncertainty.

3

We Process & Pack

Orders are consolidated, fish is processed into fillets or steaks as required, yield loss is calculated, and everything is cold-packed for delivery.

4

Fresh Same-Day Delivery

Our cold-chain logistics network delivers within 12 hours of catch, with real-time tracking all the way to the kitchen door.
