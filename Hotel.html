FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order varies by fish type. Premium varieties start at 5–10 kg while economy varieties require a minimum of 25–30 kg per order.

How fresh is the fish?

All fish is sourced daily from our trusted fishermen partners and delivered within 24 hours of catch. We never sell fish older than 36 hours.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver across Mumbai, Pune, and Nashik. We are expanding to Bangalore and Hyderabad soon. Contact us for specific area availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking. For large bulk orders above ₹1 lakh, we also offer invoice-based credit terms for verified businesses.

Can I get a customised pricing plan?

Yes! Businesses ordering above 500 kg per month qualify for negotiated wholesale pricing. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

What if I receive damaged or spoiled fish?

We have a strict quality guarantee. If you receive fish that does not meet our standards, we will offer a full replacement or refund. Report issues within 2 hours of delivery.
